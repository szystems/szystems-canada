About Us

Building Digital Success Since 2010

Szystems is a professional web development company dedicated to helping businesses thrive in the digital world. Based in Victoria, BC, we serve clients across Canada and internationally with custom websites, web applications, and digital solutions.

Founded with a mission to make professional web development accessible to businesses of all sizes, we combine technical expertise with a deep understanding of business needs to deliver solutions that drive real results.

Our Story

From a small startup to serving businesses across North America

2010

The Beginning

Szystems was founded with a simple goal: create professional, affordable web solutions for small and medium businesses. Starting as a one-person operation, we built our first websites for local businesses.

2014

Expanding Services

We expanded beyond websites into custom web applications, developing complex systems for schools, real estate companies, and e-commerce businesses. Our team grew to meet increasing demand.

2018

100+ Projects Milestone

We celebrated our 100th successful project delivery. By this time, we had built solutions across multiple industries including hospitality, education, logistics, real estate, and finance.

2025

Canadian Expansion

Szystems expanded operations to Canada, establishing our presence in Victoria, BC. We now serve businesses across Canada while maintaining our commitment to quality and personal service.
